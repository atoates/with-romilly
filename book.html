Preparing for Your Session

Before Your Appointment

  • Avoid large meals 2 hours before treatment
  • Stay hydrated throughout the day
  • Arrive 5-10 minutes early to settle in
  • Bring completed consultation form if first visit
  • Wear comfortable, loose clothing

After Your Treatment

  • Drink plenty of water to aid detoxification
  • Rest and relax for the remainder of the day
  • Avoid alcohol for 24 hours
  • Light, gentle movement is beneficial
  • Note any reactions or improvements
